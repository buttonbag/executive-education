Take your career to the next level through DePaul’s Executive Education programs. By leveraging our world-class business education, we provide professional development certificate programs for business professionals to launch or transform their careers. The DePaul Difference

DePaul Executive Education’s goal is to accelerate the development of socially responsible business leaders who thrive in a diverse and global environment.

Our open enrollment certificates were developed to create competitive advantages for individuals looking to advance their careers. We’ve employed insights from our corporate partners to create certificates that provide the real-world skills that are essential for the evolving workplace.

Our custom programs meet your organization’s talent development and educational goals and complement your existing learning and development efforts. Our top-tier faculty deliver professional development programming with a practical hands-on approach that results in immediate application in the workplace.
